Project Overview

Tunisia Go Travel is a web platform designed for targeted group training, offering tools for scheduling, tracking, and managing group sessions with ease and efficiency.

  • Group session scheduling and management
  • Progress tracking and analytics
  • Interactive dashboards for trainers and participants
  • Responsive, mobile-friendly design
  • Secure user authentication and data privacy
