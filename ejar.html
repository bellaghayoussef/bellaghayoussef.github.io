Project Overview

Ejar is a modern property rental platform designed to simplify the process of finding and managing rental properties. With a beautiful UI, advanced search, and secure booking, Ejar delivers a seamless experience for both renters and property owners.

  • Advanced property search and filtering
  • Secure online booking and payments
  • Responsive, mobile-friendly design
  • Admin dashboard for property management
  • Multi-language support
